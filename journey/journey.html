My Journey

From Liberia to the United States, it was a journey before landing in my present location. My first trip out of Liberia was in June 2003. I first landed in Lagos, Nigeria, spending one night and then to Buduburam, Ghana, where I spent five years before going back to Liberia in 2008. I finally landed in Seattle, Washington in December 2010 and I have since been stable until my next journey.
